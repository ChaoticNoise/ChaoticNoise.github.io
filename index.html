Chaotic Noise Marching Corps

is a Seattle-based renegade brass band dedicated to keeping the tradition of music, performance, energy, havoc, discipline, and sheer fun alive in the streets.

Chaotic Noise delivers high-decibel street shows that mix punk, metal, circus chaos, industrial grit, and genre-bending brass. The band's performances are wild, immersive, and intense enough to wake the dead.

Shaped by the spirit of past projects, Chaotic Noise invites you to let go, get loud, and feel the joy of shaking everything loose for a little while.
