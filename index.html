Chaotic Noise Marching Corps

is a Seattle-based renegade marching band dedicated to keeping the tradition of music, performance, energy, havoc, discipline, and sheer fun alive in the streets.

Chaotic Noise was founded by committee members and participants of HONK! Fest West, along with members of Seattle groups including Infernal Noise Brigade, Titanium Sporkestra, the Sounders FC Soundwave Band, and more. The band delivers high-decibel street shows that blend punk, metal, circus music, and more. Chaotic Noise performances are chaotic, theatrical, and loud enough to wake the dead.

Shaped by the spirit of past projects, Chaotic Noise invites you to let go, get loud, and feel the joy of shaking everything loose for a little while.
