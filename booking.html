About the Band

Formed by longtime members of Seattle’s street music scene and HONK! Fest West, Chaotic Noise fuses brass, percussion, and saxes with metal, punk, and rock energy. Our sets blend rowdy originals and ferocious covers, delivered with theatrical antics and a taste for mayhem.

Available Lineups

  • Full Corps (20–25 players): Festival stages, parades, big outdoor events
  • Strike Squad (10–15 players): Club shows, private parties, smaller stages
  • Pop-Up Crew (8–10 players): Roaming performances, surprise activations

Performance Highlights

Tech & Setup

No amps or PA needed. We're fully acoustic, mobile, and self-contained. We bring the sound, you provide the space. For longer sets, we appreciate access to water, some shade, and a short break about every 45 minutes. For larger shows with the full group, a few microphones for sousaphone, vocals, and solos can help with clarity.
